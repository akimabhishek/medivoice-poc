MediVoice AI

About the Product

An end-to-end multimodal AI reasoning engine built to parse, evaluate, and converse about complex health insurance policies with strict logical guardrails.

Core Architecture

FastAPI Gemini 2.5 Flash ChromaDB RAG Real-time TTS/STT

How to Use

  1. Sync Data: Upload a policy PDF to vectorize it into the Knowledge Base.
  2. Query: Tap the Mic to record a voice note, or type a query.
  3. Audit: Review the AI's internal reasoning chain & exact token economics.

Engineering Notes

Designed to showcase strict structural control over LLMs. The backend forces Gemini to return strict JSON, structurally separating its technical document analysis (the "Brain") from its concise, human-friendly conversational script (the "Mouth").
